# Smallest Mini Split ACs (Under 10,000 BTU) — 2026 Guide

The smallest mini splits available are 6,000 BTU units, capable of cooling rooms as small as 150 sq ft. The most common small size is 9,000 BTU, which handles 250–350 sq ft — perfect for bedrooms, home offices, server closets, and small additions. Mitsubishi's MSZ-FH09 (9K) is the best overall small mini split: 33.1 SEER2, 19 dB noise, and cold-climate heating to -13°F. For budget buyers, the MrCool DIY 12K is the smallest model in their lineup at a much lower price point.

## When You Need a Small Mini Split

Small mini splits (6K–9K BTU) are ideal for single bedrooms (150–300 sq ft), home offices (100–250 sq ft), server rooms and equipment closets (cooling heat-generating equipment), sunrooms and enclosed porches (150–250 sq ft), studio apartments (300–400 sq ft with low heat gain), and workshop areas within a larger garage.
