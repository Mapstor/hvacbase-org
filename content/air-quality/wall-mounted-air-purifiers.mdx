## Why Wall Mounting Works Better

Air purifiers perform best when elevated to breathing zone height (3–5 feet above the floor). Most people set floor units behind furniture, against walls, or in corners—all of which restrict airflow and reduce effectiveness. Wall mounting solves this by default.

## Installation Guide

### Mounting Requirements

Most wall-mounted purifiers weigh 15–30 lbs. For drywall installation: use heavy-duty drywall anchors rated for 50+ lbs (provides safety margin). For stud mounting: use standard wood screws. Mount at 3–5 feet height (center of unit at roughly chest height).

**Clearance:** Maintain 6+ inches above and below the unit, and 3+ inches on each side. The wall acts as a natural baffle behind the unit—this is accounted for in the design.

**Electrical:** You'll need an outlet within cord reach. Most wall-mount purifiers have 6–8 foot cords. A wall-mounted cord cover keeps the installation clean. Some models (Rabbit Air) offer optional recessed outlet plates for professional-looking installations.
